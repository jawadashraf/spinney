# Project Costing: Fostering & Adoption Management System (Charity / Equity)

This version of the proposal is tailored for a **Charity** client. It features a significantly reduced fixed fee, delivered over a longer timeline (4 hours per day over 24 weeks), with a future profit-sharing agreement.

---

## 1. Development Cost Summary

The development is divided into three distinct phases to ensure rapid value delivery and a focus on the Minimum Viable Product (MVP).

| Phase | Duration | Scope | Estimate |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Phase 1: MVP** | Weeks 1–8 | Foundation, Child/Family Records, Progress System, Placements | **£6,000** |
| **Phase 2: Agency Readiness** | Weeks 9–16 | Carer Portal, Digital Assessments (Form F/PAR), Shared Access | **£4,500** |
| **Phase 3: Enterprise** | Weeks 17–24 | Financials (Invoicing/Pay), Reporting, System Admin, External Portal | **£4,500** |
| **TOTAL** | **24 Weeks** | (480 Hours total @ **~£31/hr**) | **£15,000** |

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This model is provided at a **62% discount** (approx. £24,300 in "sweat equity") compared to the Consultancy model, in exchange for a **Future Revenue Share**. This reduces the effective rate from the standard £81.25/hr to **£31.25/hr**.

## 5. Revenue Share & Commercial Partnership

Given the significant reduction in upfront development costs (£24,300 labor investment), the following commercial terms apply to any future sale of the system:

*   **Gross Revenue Share:** The Developer will receive **30% of Gross Revenue** generated by any subsequent sale, license, or subscription of the software to third-party agencies. 
*   **"Gross" Definition:** The split is calculated before expenses, ensuring transparency and ease of accounting for both parties.
*   **Intellectual Property (IP):** The source code, software architecture, and all unique data structures are **Jointly Owned**. The Charity maintains exclusive branding and domain-specific knowledge, while the Developer maintains the right to utilize the technical framework for future development.
*   **Commercialisation Rights:** Both parties have the right to identify and refer potential new clients, with the Charity leading the sales process for the Fostering/Adoption sector.
